FTI - FWORK

I landed a job here as a JavaScript developer, working with both backend and frontend. My works consist developing individual services as parts of the business management suite (which utilizing the microservice architecture) and some other times, I was assigned and jumped between other service to fix bugs. Some services that I was a part of and worth mentioning are Work, Customer and Check-in. Check-in is the one service that I put in the most effort and spent the most of my time working here. It is also my very first big project that I played the major part in the development process from start to finish. I was responsible for developing 3 parts. The first one at the early stage, which is working on the core settings of the service, in particular, setting up the working time for departments of the organization, either working as “9to5” office time or in shifts. Next, I worked on developing application management, allow employees to create their application based on previously dynamic configured application templates and working time setting regarding their department. Moreover, their superiors would be notified to do the the necessary approval following the customized procedure. Lastly, I handle the part that allows shift planner to create shift schedule and accordingly generate timetable down to each individual employee for given period. Furthermore, once the schedule is set properly, shift planner have option to automate the timetable generating process itself when the current one is ended. In conclusion, throughout my time working here introduced me to a lot of new technologies and allowed myself to dive deeper into JavaScript ecosystem. I gained myself not just knowledge but also experience and I am deeply grateful for that.
