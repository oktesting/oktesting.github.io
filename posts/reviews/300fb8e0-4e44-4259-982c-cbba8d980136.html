Coconut hero

i haven't watched again yet, and i gonna do it very soon. before do that, im jotting down some of my thoughts for this movie.

Coconut hero, a movie has nothing to do with any superhero like we have known in DC or Marvel and even in the comic book movie in general. this movie is an indie one in its pure form. It sets in an ordinary world to tell an extraordinary story. A boy tried to commit suicide and he also failed to do so. his life gradually changed, neither for the better nor worse. I don't want to spoil the journey that this movie brings but extremely excited to say a quote from it: "when you think thing cannot get any worse, it definitely can".

this montage is a perfect combination of beautiful close-up shots, delicate transitions and exquisite music choice

a bit of my personal story with this movie and why i feel this is one of my all time favorite. i watch this movie out of nowhere, i did not really remember the day exactly but i recall that i first watched this one in a noon when i had nothing to do rather than lazily sitting and switching between different channels on the TV. my top choices for this scenario are Sporting channels and Movie channels. one of the best movie channel that i deeply in love with that shows various of movie regardless of genre or country, instead it just show good movies from around the world that winning or being nominated in some god-knows awards. that channel named Cinema World. i enjoyed a lot of good movies from it. some movies that i really like, but forgot the name that i'm still hoping one day i will find them again. Luckily Coconut hero was the one that i remember and actually did search for it after my first watch. this movie deserved more attention, i found very little info bout this movie on IMDb. Maybe, we should keep it that way, keep remaining this classic to me hidden to majority of the world. this movie was so interesting that when i wanna watch it again but i could not found on any big ILLIGAL streaming sites. However right when i was disappointed bout this situation i found it on a very random unknown sketchy site. the problem is that version is raw with no sub. i utilize the built-in finding sub function of VLC and managed to find one, but that Eng sub sucked so bad. an idea struck my mind that i decide to do the Vie Sub myself based on the already perfect synchronized but inaccurate EngSub. i did the Vie Sub in one afternoon and night in my dormitory's room. my roommates even asked me if i was coding in the afternoon Sunday when they see me doing that. the process of making it was really fun when i was actively listening and watching scene by scene over and over again. that when i discover many things i missed before and understand the hidden meanings. the Vie sub was finished and i felt really proud of one of my very first accomplishment as a side project.
