The Batman

let’s talk batshit (ba[t]d joke intended, and shall continue)

yes, i admit that i am not a hardcore batman fan. i watched some batman movies before and had mixed feelings about them, some are good, some are not so much and even it’s good then maybe that batman is not always the center that had me sold. some batman i really like at the visually big-bad-ass looking and mentally-paranoia like batflex, some had the most ground-up, realistic and serious story and journey like christian bale version. some with the dirty dark nostalgia 80s-90s vibe that i couldn’t explain like keaton one. (man, lately i rewatch some of the val kilmer’s batman videos too)

and why am i saying all this shit, bc i think this batman had the all every elements aboved combined. that’s all i have to say. love the badass gritty look, love the exhilarating story

i came to the movie as a blank canvas, this movie painted all over it with so much colors all over the place and that’s the good thing tho cuz the colors in this one fucking top-notch. what do u imagine with a city that’s 80% night all the time, chance of raining is always above 101% and combine with the lighting from lamppost and skyscraper. yeah boi, one ticket to gotham please, i brought the raincoat.

the batman took no time to pump my adrenaline up to the max when mr. vengeance brutally beat the living batshit out of the gang member. i saw it in the trailer but still stunt by it. that is just the beginning cuz in the middle of the movie, the car chase scene with penguin took the adrenaline in me to the next level, i remember watching it and my heart pounding hard as shit, like i could have a stroke at anytime with the visually explosion and sounding from the speaker. feel like i am the penguin the the upside down car looking at the intimidating figure walking closer. damn good. lastly, at the end, batman popping pill (how crazy this ‘taking substance to become stronger’ trope is used so much in superhero movie) and again beat a guy face to pulp. i really thought to myself a 2 questions:

  • is this guy batman beat up so bad, he became the Joker, just look at his torn face. that will be wild origin
  • is this guy is the first victim of the the gang that batman saved from in the opening. if this’s true then damn, dark path spares for no one

the soundtrack is clichély EPIC. it’s like the reinforcement for the movie’s breathtaking and immersive quality. something ‘is definitely’ in the fucking’ way. man i never thought a nirvana song like this could fit so well in a batman movie, not in the wildest thought. like playing this song the climax of dark knight rises then the reaction will be: “bitch, u better be joe king” as maddy💅 said. i was jumping in my gut and humming along when this song kicked in the beginning and also in the end of the movie man. another great track is the batman theme, oh boi, i walked out of the theater while this track is still on and feel “I’M BATMAN🦇” like i could punch the shit out of who the fuck standing in my way.

i’m not gonna say much into the plot cuz u have to see it to believe (and also it’s 3 HOURS LONG), that mr. riddler had laid the masterplan, waiting for ya. his mastermind pulling strings even on batman as his puppet (and also an idol vigilante figure). riddler with his plan basically had naïve batman chasing and doing all of the dirty muscle works for him, always one step behind when discover any piece of truth that was intentionally given (yeah, behold, the greatest detective in his “sophomore😂” year). the convo between batman and riddler went apeshit(yes, i’m referring alfred😁) when riddler challenge batman’s secret identity (or not). this sickhead riddling mf is very scary psychopath with a baby face (cute). loveeeeeeeee paul dano, feel bad for every movie starring him i had watched before like ruby spark or prisoner.

haha gotchu batty, ily tho 😳😊

ok, i watched the deleted scene and as the conversation at the end between the riddler and a mysterious prisoner, confirms that it’s the Joker. The hype is real man, oh my gwuad. the interaction joker had with batman and riddler proves that this mf is on top of the game bro :)) keoghan, LET’SSSSSSSSS GOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOO (*apeshit screaming intensifies)
